Privacy Policy

for Null Talisman Studios Applications & Games

Effective Date: November 25, 2025

Null Talisman Studios ("We," "Us," or "Our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users ("User" or "You"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and disclose information when you use our mobile applications and games (the "Services").

1. Information We Collect

We primarily collect two types of information through our Services:

1.1. Non-Personal Information (Automatically Collected)

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect non-personal information about your device and usage patterns. This data is collected to improve the Services, provide support, and facilitate advertising. This may include:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system (e.g., Android, iOS), hardware information, CPU/memory, device language, and time zone.
  • Device Identifiers: Advertising identifiers (IDFA or GAID), IP address (used to infer approximate country or city-level location, but not precise GPS coordinates).
  • Usage and Log Data: Game progress, scores, achievements, session duration, preferred settings, time of use, and interaction with in-game advertisements.

1.2. Personal Information (Voluntarily Provided)

We only collect personal information if you voluntarily provide it, typically when you contact us for support or feedback. This may include your email address and any information you provide in your support request or feedback form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and provide the core functionality of our Services.
  • To analyze and improve our games, troubleshoot technical issues, and understand user trends.
  • To serve contextual and, where permitted by law or consent, personalized advertisements to fund the free Services.
  • For internal analytics, research, and business intelligence, often using aggregated and anonymized data.
  • To respond to your support requests, inquiries, and feedback.
  • To detect and prevent fraud and enforce our terms and conditions.

3. Sharing and Disclosure of Information

We do not sell your Personal Information. We may share information with third parties only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share non-personal data with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, game development tools (e.g., Unity), and analytics (e.g., Google Analytics, Firebase).
  • Advertising Partners: We use third-party advertising networks to display ads in our Services. These partners may collect and use non-personal data (like device identifiers) to serve ads and measure campaign effectiveness.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose information if required to do so by law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

Note: Our advertising and analytics partners operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review their policies for more information on their data handling practices.

4. Children's Privacy

Our Services are generally intended for users aged 13 and over (16 and over for users in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under these ages.

If we learn that we have collected personal data from a child under the applicable age without verifiable parental consent, we will take reasonable steps to delete it promptly. If you believe that we might have any information from or about a child under the applicable age, please contact us at the email provided below.

5. Your Rights and Opt-Out Options

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR, CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data. You have options regarding data collection:

  • Opt-Out of Personalized Ads: You can limit the use of your device's advertising identifier for interest-based advertising by adjusting the privacy settings on your device (e.g., "Limit Ad Tracking" on iOS or "Opt out of Ads Personalization" on Android).
  • Exercise Data Rights: To make a request regarding your data rights (e.g., access or deletion), please contact us directly using the contact information below.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
